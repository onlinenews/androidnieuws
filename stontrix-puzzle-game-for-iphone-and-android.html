Stontrix puzzle game for iPhone and Android

Stontrix is a deceptively simple game but with many possibilities. It is about changing stone positions by sliding a finger to align at least three stones of the same color. Alignment can be done in any direction, either vertically, horizontally or diagonally. Aligned stones are then eliminated in order to achieve the objective of the game: remove as many stones as quickly as possible and avoid them filling the screen.

The stones of the highest line can be placed in any position within the game screen. The movement of the rest of the stones will be to the closest position in order to complete a combination. The game works with coins that can be obtained during the game, they can also be bought or received for watching video ads within the game.

But thatâ€™s not all; Stontrix stones can come with surprise and help us out. For example, as soon as you align stones that come with coins with others of the same color you get more virtual coins that add more time for playing, or allow buying power ups to help you in the game. Stones with a clock give more seconds to play if lined with the same colored stones, or stones with bombs if aligned with the same color will explode and destroy those that are around.

There are also stones that complicate the game for us, such as stones with a lock that apart from not disappearing the first time aligned with the stones of the same color also get blocked and you need to align them twice to remove them, or stones with a skull that cannot be aligned with the stones of the same color. If you let that happen you lose a game.

These are some of the possibilities of Stontrix, so download the game and find out for yourself what other surprises Stontrix has got for you. What you will discover for sure is fun on any level! Always carry the game with you and keep overcoming its challenges!
