Battleship - Gratuite | appxoid

A board game classic receives cool new features. In addition to ships you are given an extended arsenal which includes planes, sea mines and air defense.
Put your ships in battle order, provide them with protection using planes and mines, and launch strikes on the opponent’s fleet from sea and air.

Battleship is a lively animated game with graphic effects.

Game modes:
1. Android mode – choose your computer opponent level and get bonus points for beating him.
2. Two players mode – Want to play against your friend but don’t have a second phone? No problem! Separately arrange your ships, planes and mines and start the battle.
3. Bluetooth mode – challenge your friends and colleagues via Bluetooth.

Get bonus points for each victory and use them to buy planes, mines and air defense.

*****
It’s high time to find out who is the best at Battleship!

*****
To play via bluetooth, do the following:

1) Click the "Play via Bluetooth" button
2) Enable Bluetooth on your phone.
3) Click the "Show Me" button on both phones.
4) One of the player clicks the "Search" button, selects the second player phone from the devices list.
5) If you can not join in, then the other player does point 4.
