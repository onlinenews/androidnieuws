In pictures What’s in the cards for Android 5.0?

What’s in the cards for Android 5.0? Android smartphone and tablet makers are notorious for not updating their devices to the latest versions of the OS in a timely fashion — if ever. But it appears Google has been deploying a stealthy strategy to mitigate this problem somewhat: They have released new apps or revised many of the ones that come with Android. For users, this means an improvement in the overall Android experience.
