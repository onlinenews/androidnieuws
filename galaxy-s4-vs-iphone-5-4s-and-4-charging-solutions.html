Galaxy S4 vs. iPhone 5, 4S and 4 charging solutions

Today, we wanted to take a look at some charging solutions for the Samsung Galaxy S4 vs. iPhone 5, 4S and 4 that can be seen in a number of videos. The Galaxy S4 supports the Qi standard, so one of the most innovative chargers for this smartphone would be a Qi Wireless Charger.

We have featured a video below this article that looks at four wireless chargers utilizing the Qi standard, and as such supported by Samsung’s Galaxy S4 along with other phones like the Galaxy S3, Nexus 4 and Droid DNA. This video runs for just over 4 minutes and quickly delivers some chargers you might want to consider for your Galaxy S4.

When it comes to charging your iPhone you will find a massive range of accessories on the official Apple Store. These range from USB chargers and battery cases to docks and extreme charging stations. You can see the range offered through Apple on this store page, but today we wanted to feature a couple of solutions in two more videos below this article.

Samsung Galaxy S4 running Android 4.3 and iPhone 5 with iOS 7 – some of our readers might wonder what the differences are in charging solutions for Galaxy S4 vs. iPhone 5 and 4S/4, but there’s plenty of solutions catering for Apple and Samsung phones. This is perfect if your family uses both an iPhone and Galaxy S4.

The second video below looks at the TUDIA Dual Car Charger and in their review they demo an iPhone 5 with iOS 7 and Galaxy S4 running Android 4.3 Jelly Bean. This charger is pretty simple when compared to some of the other options, but it will cater to both brands when you are traveling in a car.

In the last video you will see an option designed just for Apple’s iPhone 5 and iPhone 4S/4. The Universal Audio Dock will charge both new iPhone 5’s with lighting port and older 30-pin iPhone 4 and 4S models. It also features a Bluetooth speaker, so again this is handy for families with new and older iPhones, which is exactly how it works in my family with 2 iPhone 4S’s and one iPhone 5.
