Phata Poster Nikhla Hero First Look Released

The makers of upcoming film Phata Poster Nikhla Hero have released its First Look now. The poster features Shahid Kapoor, who is playing the lead role in the film.

Well, there’s an interesting trivia you would love to know about! For the movie, Shahid’s first scene required him to tear the poster and his introduction scene will be quite dynamic. Since the makers felt the movie Ajab Prem Ki Gazab Kahaani was lucky for them, Shahid Kapoor was told to tear the poster of the movie and inspite of having the option of a body double, Shahid did the stunt all by himself!

Produced by Ramesh Taurani and directed by Rajkumar Santoshi, the film also stars Ileana D’Cruz. Phata Poster Nikhla Hero will release on 20th Sept, 2013.
