Put Math Skills on a Test in a Race Against Friends – Math Duel 2 Player Math Game

Source: Peaksel D.O.O.

Nis, Serbia and Montenegro, July 12, 2013 --(PR.com)-- Peaksel LLC has just launched a second-in-the-row 2-player game, which combines quick fingers, and a good math knowledge in one free game known as Math Duel: 2 Player Math Game.

The main feature of the game is the split-screen interface that actually makes this game a game for two players. The goal of the game is to be the first player who will reach 10 points. With every correct answer, the player gets 1 point, but also, the player may lose 1 point if he or she gives the wrong answer. Another catch is that only the player who gives the correct answer first gets one point. So, not only do players need good math skills but also very quick fingers. There are four different levels of difficulty – easy, medium, hard and expert, so the game can be adjusted for little children, for teens and for adults.

“Our motto is ‘Two can have fun at the same time!’ All the members of our team are competitive people and we enjoy playing multiplayer games. Here we wanted to make our players employ their brains just a little bit, so we’ve made this game a math game. My niece gave it a five-star mark, and she’s very harsh when it comes to games, so I believe this one is pretty good,” said Djordje, CEO of the company.

Math duel is at the same time fun and educational game. It makes brain pumping all the time. The game can be downloaded here: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mathduel2playersgame.mathgame
