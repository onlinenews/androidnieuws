Toshiba AT7-A Benchmark Results Leaked Toshiba AT7-A Sports 7 inch Display and Runs on Android 4.2.2

Toshiba AT7-A Benchmark Results Leaked: Toshiba AT7-A Sports 7 inch Display and Runs on Android 4.2.2

Toshiba AT7-A is the upcoming 7 inch tablet from Toshiba.  The tablet will run on Android 4.2.2 Jelly bean operating system, according to benchmark results.  It will be powered by 1.6 GHz processor.  There is no official confirmation from Toshiba regarding the tablet.

Tokyo: GFX Benchmark results of a new 7 inch tablet were spotted online.  The tablet is from Toshiba, a Japan based company.  The results of benchmarks were published showing the tablet which is powered by 1.6 GHz processor.

Toshiba’s new 7 inch tablet runs on Android 4.2.2 Jelly Bean operating system, show the benchmark results.  The handset is powered by 1.6 GHz processor and there is also Mali-400 MP GPU. The display resolution of the handset is 1024 x 600 pixels.  There is no official announcement about the upcoming tablet from Toshiba.  It is tentatively called AT7-A.

Toshiba’s AT7-A upcoming tablet scored 17,639 on AnTuTu benchmarks.  The device may not be very impressive when it comes to performance.  But it the 1.6 GHz CPU is combined with four separate cores, the tablet will be one of the best entry level tablets available.  Toshiba’s recent tablet launches are Excite Write and so on.

Toshiba however has not said anything about its upcoming tablet AT7-A.  The detailed leaks of the handset can only b expected after the official announcement of the device from the company.  Thus the release date of the handset is also not known till now neither the details of the price.

Leaked specs of Toshiba AT7-2

The tablet runs on 4.2.2 Jelly bean operating system 7 inch display 1024 x 600 pixel resolution 1.6 GHz processor ARM Mali-400 graphics

Toshiba already launched Excite Pure, Pro and Write tablets.  All these were also based on Android operating system.  The displays of all these tablets were 10.1 inch and were powered by Tegra processors.  The prices of these tablets were $299.99, $499.99 and $599.99 respectively for Pure, Pro and Write tablets.
