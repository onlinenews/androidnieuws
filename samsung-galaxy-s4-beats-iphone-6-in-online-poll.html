Samsung Galaxy S4 Beats iPhone 6 In Online Poll

Our readers has decided, Samsung Galaxy S4 inevitably the smartphone they’ll choose over iPhone 6.

Samsung Galaxy S4 Wins Online Poll Against iPhone 6

The aforementioned smartphones are expected for unveiling next year, has been widely rumored to have wide range of new technology patents ironically after both companies’ legal patent dispute in the past.

Apple patents suggest the “iPhone 6 design might hide external-facing components such as the camera and flash from view using a polymer-dispersed liquid crystal window which can change opacity on demand.” This design makeover would be purely aesthetic, although anyone would hope the camera quality would not be reduced.

A lot of people using the iPhone have been complaining about the CPU resources having less power than  smartphones powered by Android such as Samsung Galaxy series.

But, it can be argued that even with quad-cores, it doesn’t push the limit of the hardware as it all depends on how the application utilize the operating system.

It is worth mentioning that if you monitor the core usage on your quad-core Android phone, you will occasionally notice that the extra cores will be turned off even during a 3D game.
