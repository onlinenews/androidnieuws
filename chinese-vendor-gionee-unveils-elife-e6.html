Chinese vendor Gionee unveils Elife E6

MUMBAI: China-based handset maker Gionee has announced the launch of a smartphone Gionee Elife E6 in Chinese and India market.


The product features android 4.2, Â 5-inch OGS(One-Glass-Solution) AMOLED display with a resolution of 1080x1920 pixels, 1.5GHz quad-core processor, 2GB RAM, 32GB internal storage, 13 MP rear camera with auto focus and LED flash, 5 MP front facing camera, Wi-Fi, GPS, Bluetooth, FM radio and 2020mAh battery.


Gionee Communication vice president William Lu said, "With its intuitive and user interface, we have developed the Elife E6 with the customers' interest at heart. Supplying what is truly important to them in terms of quality, performance and affordability - all in a stylish and sophisticated design."


The phone is priced at CNY 2699 (Rs. 26,250 approximately). It will be available in China starting mid-July and in India after August this year.
