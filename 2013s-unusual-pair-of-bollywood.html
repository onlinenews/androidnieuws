2013′s Unusual Pair Of Bollywood

Not every Bollywood pair has the chemistry of SRK-Kajol. In recent times, as realistic cinema has plundered the Bollywood’s mainstream film scenario the quintessential typical Romantic jodis of Bollywood have suffered a beating from the newer pairs that have been lined up.

Koimoi takes a look at the most quirky pairings of 2013 :

Bipasha Basu-Nawazuddin Siddiqui

Bipasha Basu And Nawazuddin Siddiqui

Known for her chemistry with the more tall, dark and obviously handsome men, Bipasha Basu’s pairing with Nawazuddin Siddiqui was the most surprising element of the film Aatma. Though the film did not work overtly well at the box office, the jodi will be remembered for a long time as one of the year’s most strikingly different pairing.

Randeep Hooda-Rani Mukerji

Randeep Hooda And Rani Mukerji in Bombay Talkies Movie Stills

Belonging to absolutely different dimensions of cinema, their pairing in Bombay Talkies was unusual. Randeep Hooda and Rani Mukerji though managed to click off quite well, it is always easier to remember Rani as an actress who can show a good chemistry with any actor on screen and add charm to it.

Sanjay Dutt-Prachi Desai

Sanjay Dutt And Prachi Desai Policegiri Movie Stills

Now, this was a jodi which actually made no sense to me! Prachi Desai being years younger than Sanjay Dutt, their jodi was out of place and particularly odd. The chemistry had nothing striking about it and the age difference between the two loomed a little too conspicuously in Policegiri. This one turned out to be quite a disaster.

Sunny Deol-Kangana Ranaut

Sunny Deol And Kangana Ranaut in I Love New Year Movie Stills

With his action blazing screens, his chemistry with women usually go for a toss. But not when he is paired opposite the beautiful Kangana Ranaut. Sunny Deol and Kangana are slated to appear together in I Love New Year which is constantly getting delayed, but for sure their chemistry will be one thing to look out for in the film besides the usual elements.

Vidya Balan-Farhan Akhtar

Farhan Akhtar And Vidya Balan

Farhan Akhtar as Milkha Singh is all over the headline but in his next film he will is paired opposite Vidya Balan. Though Vidya has shown adequate promise as an actor and delivering wonderful chemistry with every actor she has worked with, it will be a refreshing to see Vidya and Farhan bicker as a normal couple in Shaadi Ke Side Effects!

Randeep Hooda-Alia Bhatt

Randeep Hooda And Alia Bhatt in Highway Movie Stills

Randeep Hooda known for his rustic looks and Alia Bhatt who epitomizes innocence with her cutesy face is another unusual pairing. To be seen together in Imtiaz Ali’s Highway, their contrasting demeanor might compliment well on screen if executed in such a fashion. But the age difference again, might glower strongly which could deter. In the end, it is all about how well will Imtiaz Ali handle their chemistry.

Saif Ali Khan-Kalki Koechlin

Saif Ali Khan And Kalki Koechlin

Sticking mainly to offbeat films, Kalki’s first tryst with mainstream Bollywood was Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani and she will further it by romancing Saif Ali Khan in Raj-D.K’s next film that has a script Kalki couldn’t refuse. A very catchy pairing, knowing Kalki’s acting skills and Saif’s adaptability, this might again be a jodi to look out for in 2013.
