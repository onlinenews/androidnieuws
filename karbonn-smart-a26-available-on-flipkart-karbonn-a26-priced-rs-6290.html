Karbonn Smart A26 Available on Flipkart: Karbonn A26 Priced Rs. 6,290

Karbonn Smart A26 dual SIM phone running on Android Jelly Bean operating system is now available on Flipkart.  The online retailer is offering the handset for Rs. 6,290. Color options are black and white.

Mumbai: Flipkart listed Karbonn A26 smartphone.  The handset is offered by the online retailer for a price of Rs. 6,290.

Karbonn Smart A26 sports 5 inch FWVGA screen.  It also supports dual SIM and comes equipped with dual camera.  The handset runs on Android Jelly Bean 4.1 operating system. This system does not support 3G but only 2G – GPRS and EDGE.  Memory provided is 4 GB internal storage and 512 MB of RAM. It comes with dual camera – there is 5 megapixel rear camera that features LED flash for capturing images. The front facing VGA camera can be used for taking self portraits. The whole device gets its power from 2000 mAh battery.  It supports Wi-Fi, micro USB and Bluetooth.

Karbonn A26 Key Specifications

This handset sports 5 inch display The display resolution is capacitive touchscreen Display resolution is 854 x 480 pixels Body dimensions of the handset are 74 x 146 x 8.65 mm It is powered by 1 GH dual core processor The handset runs on Android v4.1.2 Jelly Bean operating system Smart A26 supports dual SIM It is provided with 4 GB internal storage 512 MB of RAM Memory can be expanded up to 32 GB via micro SD It comes equipped with 5 megapixel rear camera with LED flash There is also a secondary VGA camera the battery under the hood is 2000 mAh connectivity features include WiFi, WAP, Bluetooth 3.5mm audio jack G-sensor, light and proximity sensors FM radio

Flipkart is offering Karbonn Smart A26 with one year manufacturing warranty on the phone and there is 6 month warranty on the accessories. The online retailer is offering the handset in white and black color options. The online retailer claims that the handset will be delivered to the customers within 7-8 days from the day of receiving the order.  Cash on deliver, EMI option and 30 day replacement guarantee options are also offered to the customers.
