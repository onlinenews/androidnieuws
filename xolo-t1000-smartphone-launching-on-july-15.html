Xolo T1000 smartphone launching on July 15

New Delhi, July 14 (TruthDive): Xolo has confirmed that it will start shipping the product Xolo T1000 starting July 15.

Earlier, Xolo, a division of Lava mobiles had recently announced the launch of Xolo T1000, Android based smartphone. The phone is already available for pre-order on Flipkart for Rs 16,499.

Xolo T1000 features a 4.7-inch IPS HD capacitive touchscreen which has a resolution of 1280 x 720 pixels giving it a pixel density of 293 ppi that produces crisp and clear pictures, making gaming and watching movies fun.

The display is based on one-glass solution (OGS) technology. Xolo T1000 has a Tegra 3 quad-core 1.5 GHz Nvidia processor and it runs on Android 4.1.1 Jelly Bean operating system. It has 1GB RAM.

The device is packed by internal storage capacity of 4GB which can be expanded by another 32GB with the help of a memory card.  The smartphone comes with an 8 megapixel rear camera with BSI sensor, LED flash, 1080p video recording and a 2 megapixel front-facing camera. It is powered with 2,000mAh battery and is powered enough to deliver roughly 11 hours of talk time or 227 hours of standby.

The smartphone weighs 167 grams and it will support Bluetooth v3 as well as Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n. The other features include active noise cancellation and 3G support for 21 Mbps speed.
