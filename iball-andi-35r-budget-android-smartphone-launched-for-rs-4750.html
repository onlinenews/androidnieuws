iBall Andi 3.5r budget Android smartphone launched for Rs 4,750

Indian smartphone vendor iBall has launched a new entry-level Android smartphone called the iBall Andi 3.5r. The 3.5-inch 3G-enabled smartphone is available on e-commerce sites and retail stores around the country for Rs 4,750.

Talking of the specifications, the Andi 3.5r sports a 3.5-inch HVGA display and is powered by a 1GHz Cortex A9 processor. Other features included are a 5-megapixel rear camera, VGA camera at the front for video calling, expandable memory to up to 32GB using a microSD card and a 1,300mAh battery.

Connectivity-wise, the Andi 3.5r features dual-SIM card slot, 3G support, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 4.0, GPS and A-GPS and on the software front, the device runs on an outdated Android 2.3.6 Gingerbread. Priced at Rs 4,750, the Andi 3.5r will be up against the likes of Intex Aqua Marvel+ and the Magicon M2 Elegante among others.
