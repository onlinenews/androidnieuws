Spice launched Pinnacle FHD with 1080p full HD display and Android 4.2 Jelly Bean

Spice has launched yet another new smartphone in the market and this one is called the Pinnacle FHD. This is an Android smartphone which sports a 1080p full HD resolution display and it is priced at just Rs, 16,990.



The Spice Pinnacle FHD sports a 5-inch 1080p full HD resolution display, for this price it is an impressive offer. It is powered by a quad-core processor clocked at 1.5GHz and includes 1GB of RAM. The smartphone also includes 8GB of built-in storage, which can be further expanded by an additional 32GB using a microSD card.Â  This is a dual-SIM capable smartphone and both the SIM card slots support 3G connectivity. Users also get 2GB of free data on Spice Cloud.

The Pinnacle FHD comes pre-loaded with Android 4.2 Jelly Bean and it sports an 8 megapixel rear camera with dual LED flash along with a 2 megapixel front facing camera. It is equipped with a 2,100 mAh battery and supports Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, GPS/aGPS connectivity.

The Spice Pinnacle FHD is currently available for pre-booking on Saholic, apparently the device will be available in retail stores starting July 20 which also the date when it will start shipping to all those who pre-ordered the smartphone.

The device is likely to face tough competition from the Micromax Canvas 4 which was recently launched with a Rs. 17,999 price tag as well as the XOLO Play which has a 15,999 price tag.
