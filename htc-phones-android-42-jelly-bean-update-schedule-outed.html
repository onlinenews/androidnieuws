HTC Phones Android 4.2 Jelly Bean Update Schedule Outed


According to a source the HTC EVO 4G LTE, HTC Droid DNA, HTC One X+ and the HTC One X+ LTE will all be getting updated with Jelly Bean 4.2 and it will include Sense 5, identical to the HTC One.

HTC are yet to launch an update to Jelly Bean 4.2 for their handsets. The HTC One includes Jelly Bean 4.2 out of the box. Nothing has been said about which handsets will receive the update later on either. However due to information being leaked, we now have an idea of what is going to happen.

In the past the source @LlabTooFer has always been correct with his claims and he has stated that it won’t be long till the HTC One will get updated to 4.2.2.

He also said that the HTC EVO 4G LTE (Jewel), Droid DNA (DLX_WL), HTC One X+ (Evita) and HTC One X+ LTE (Evitare) would all be updated to Jelly Bean 4.2 in the future as well. The handsets will also be getting Sense 5. HTC haven’t confirmed any of this information yet as things stand at the moment these are all just rumours.

The source didn’t tell us which other HTC devices could be getting Sense, such as the HTC One S and the HTC One X. HTC have announced that at some point over the next several months the handsets will be getting a number of the Sense 5 features. Things are all a bit uncertain right now as we also have no idea when the devices will be updated to Jelly Bean.

Sense 5 was leaked on the DRoid DNA and the source said previously that the handset would be getting this, but didn’t mention a thing about the Jelly Bean 4.2 update.

Right now all we want is some conformation from HTC.
