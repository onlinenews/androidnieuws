Lady Gaga ARTPOP app

Over 58 million people were given a chance to see the news from Lady Gaga about her ARTPOP app this week. This is the number of people that follow the pop stars Facebook page, which includes a post on Friday about the release date for a new single, album and app.

The Lady Gaga ARTPOP app was detailed with the simple sharing of an image and you can see the photo Lady Gaga shared on her Facebook page above. Within this image and next to the photo, you will find details on the August 19 release for the single. Then another date detailed September 1 as the day you can pre-order her ARTPOP album and app, and finally the release would be a couple of months later on November 11.

Lady Gaga’s ARTPOP app was built by TechHAUS and is said to be a “visual engineering system” that combines fashion, art, music and technology with social media. It should be creative and going by the way Lady Gaga likes to innovative, we expect something special and a release on Android, iPhone and other iOS devices.
