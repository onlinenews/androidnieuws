Boost Mobile now offering Samsung Galaxy Prevail II




Boost Mobile announced on Thursday that the Samsung Galaxy Prevail II is now available through the prepaid carrier. Priced at $179.99, the phone runs Android 4.1 Jelly Bean and features a 1.4GHz processor, 5-megapixel rear camera, 1.3-megapixel front-facing camera, and 4-inch display.

Boost Mobile offers an Android Unlimited plan for $55 that allows for shrinking bill payments. For every six bills you pay on time (does not need to be consecutive), your bill drops by $5 per month.
