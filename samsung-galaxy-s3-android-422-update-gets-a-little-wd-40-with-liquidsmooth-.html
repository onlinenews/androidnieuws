Samsung Galaxy S3 Android 4.2.2 Update Gets A Little WD-40 With LiquidSmooth ROM


If you were one of the owners of the Samsung Galaxy S3 and you had been waiting for the release of the Jelly Bean update then you might have thought that it would never arrive. However it eventually did and now the wait is on for the next version of Jelly Bean.

Of course if you are in the US it could take some time before it arrives and with this in mind you might want to consider putting a custom ROM onto the device.

One of the best at the moment is LiquidSmooth as it is based on Jelly Bean 4.2 and as such it comes with all the features. These include things like lock screen sliders, boost to speed, buttons and hardware and software keys. All of these are customisable. There are also brand new notification power widgets, navigation bar widgets, a navbar ring and status bar, which are transparent. The battery light along with the notification bar is also customisable.

The ROM comes with another superb feature in in the shape of the Chronus widget, while the stings panel can also be customised. The only downside to the ROM is that for the time being it will only work with the Cricket version of the Samsung Galaxy S3.
