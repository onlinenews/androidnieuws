Gionee Elife E6 launched with 1080p Retina display, quad-core processor and 13-megapixel camera, coming to India post August
Thursday, 11 July 2013 (2 hours ago)

Following the launch of Gionee Elife E5 smartphone for Rs 19,999 in India, the Chinese vendor has today announced the Elife E6, which was rumored for long now. The phone features a full HD Retina display and will be available post August in India. Talking about the specifications, the phone weighs in at 128g, is 6.18 mm thin, and has a 2.54mm narrow edge. This is a single-SIM phone which has a 5-inch full HD Retina display with a 1080p resolution and pixel density of 441 ppi.
