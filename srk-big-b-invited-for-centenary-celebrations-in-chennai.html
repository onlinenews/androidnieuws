SRK-Big B Invited For Centenary Celebrations In Chennai

The South Indian Film Chamber Of Commerce has invited Bollywood superstars Amitabh Bachchan and Shah Rukh Khan in their celebrations of Indian Cinema’s Centenary year to be held from 1st to 3rd of September. This will be a cue for two of Bollywood’s biggest actors to share podium on a crucial occasion.

Amitabh Bachchan And Shah Rukh Khan

The 3 day event is expected to bring together the entire South Indian Film Fraternity at Nehru Indoor Stadium, Chennai, along with the Chief ministers of Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh.

Sources confirmed that besides Big B and SRK’s presence, music composers Illayaraja or A.R Rahman might make a special song for the final day of the event.
