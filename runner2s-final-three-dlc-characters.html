Runner2′s final three DLC characters

Runner2′s final three DLC characters

By Thomas Godden on July 11th, 2013

One android test subject, one unnamed adventurer and one transparent antihero.

With just a few hours until the Good Friends Character Pack arrives, Gaijin Games have revealed the final three characters that will be joining BIT.TRIP Presents Runner2: Future Legend of Rhythm Alien.

Day one offered Raz and Quote while day two delivered Josef and Dr. Fetus but day three reveals Atlas (Steam exclusive), the charismatic little robot from Portal 2, the Spelunky Guy from Spelunky and finally an alternate version of CommanderVideo created when he was hit by the un-fusion beam at the start of Runner2, Invisible CommanderVideo. It’s a shame that costumes don’t alter gameplay but they’re hardly expensive.

The Good Friends Character Pack will be available later on today on PC for no more than $3.00 (No UK price as of yet) and hit Xbox 360, PlayStation 3 and Nintendo Wii U in the near future.
